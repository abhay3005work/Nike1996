THE SHOT

The iconic moment when Michael Jordan elevated over Craig Ehlo to hit the series-winning shot in the first round of the 1989 playoffs.

THE SHRUG

In the 1992 NBA Finals, Jordan hit six 3-pointers in the first half against Portland. After the sixth, he shrugged as if to say, "I don't know what's happening."

FLU GAME

Game 5 of the 1997 NBA Finals. Battling severe illness, Jordan scored 38 points and hit the game-winning three-pointer against the Utah Jazz.

THE LAST SHOT

Jordan's final shot as a Chicago Bull, hitting the game-winner in Game 6 of the 1998 NBA Finals to secure his sixth championship.
